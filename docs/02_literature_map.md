# Literature Map

## A. Optimization foundations

- Convex optimization: Boyd and Vandenberghe.
- Variational analysis: Rockafellar and Wets.
- Gamma convergence: Dal Maso.
- Mirror descent and dual averaging.
- Primal-dual algorithms.

## B. Probability and statistics

- Measure-theoretic probability.
- Empirical measures.
- Weak convergence.
- Concentration inequalities.
- Statistical learning theory.
- Wasserstein convergence.

## C. Optimal transport

- Kantorovich formulation.
- Wasserstein distance.
- Entropic optimal transport.
- Sinkhorn algorithm.
- Benamou-Brenier dynamic formulation.
- Schrödinger bridge.

## D. Diffusion models

Core concepts:

- Score function \(\nabla_x \log p_t(x)\).
- Denoising score matching.
- Forward SDE.
- Reverse-time SDE.
- Probability flow ODE.
- Sampling error.

Recommended starting papers:

- Score-Based Generative Modeling through Stochastic Differential Equations.
- Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models.
- Maximum Likelihood Training of Score-Based Diffusion Models.

## E. Flow matching

Core concepts:

- Vector field learning.
- Continuity equation.
- Conditional probability paths.
- Conditional Flow Matching.
- OT-CFM.

Recommended starting papers:

- Flow Matching for Generative Modeling.
- Improving and Generalizing Flow-Based Generative Models with Minibatch Optimal Transport.

## F. Schrödinger bridge

Core concepts:

- Entropic OT.
- Path-space KL.
- Stochastic control.
- Bridge between two marginal distributions.

Recommended starting papers:

- Simulation-Free Schrödinger Bridges via Score and Flow Matching.

## G. Distributional optimization and alignment

Core concepts:

- KL-regularized optimization over probability distributions.
- Reward/preference optimization.
- Dual averaging.
- Sampling guarantees.

Recommended starting papers:

- Direct Distributional Optimization for Provable Alignment of Diffusion Models.
