# PhD Research Proposal Outline

## Title

Variational Convergence and Optimality Conditions for Entropy-Regularized Distributional Optimization with Applications to Diffusion and Flow Generative Models

## 1. Introduction

Explain why optimization on probability spaces is central to modern generative models.

## 2. Background and literature review

Cover:

- Variational analysis.
- Optimization and KKT theory.
- Probability/statistical learning.
- Optimal transport.
- Diffusion and flow generative models.

## 3. Research problem

Define the main distributional optimization problem.

## 4. Research objectives

1. Establish existence and optimality conditions.
2. Prove variational stability under empirical/minibatch approximation.
3. Develop primal-dual algorithms.
4. Apply the framework to diffusion/flow toy models.

## 5. Methodology

- Convex and variational analysis.
- Probability and weak convergence.
- Optimal transport.
- Numerical simulations.

## 6. Expected contributions

- Theoretical KKT/duality results.
- Stability theorem.
- Algorithmic convergence theorem.
- Toy experimental validation.

## 7. Timeline

Year 1: foundations and first theorem.
Year 2: primal-dual algorithm and empirical stability.
Year 3: generative model application and thesis writing.
