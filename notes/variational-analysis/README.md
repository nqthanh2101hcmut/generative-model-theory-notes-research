# Variational Analysis Notes

Goal: understand existence, convergence, and stability of solutions to optimization problems.

## Main topics

1. Lower semicontinuity and coercivity.
2. Existence of minimizers.
3. Gamma convergence and epi convergence.
4. Stability of minimizers.
5. Application to empirical and minibatch approximations.

## Minimum mastery checklist

- [ ] Can prove existence of a minimizer using compactness and lower semicontinuity.
- [ ] Can state the liminf and recovery sequence conditions for Gamma convergence.
- [ ] Can explain why variational convergence is useful for approximation of optimization problems.
- [ ] Can connect empirical approximation to stability of minimizers.
